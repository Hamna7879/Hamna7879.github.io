    ABOUT US

    Ameidic Biotech Pvt Ltd (India), led by founder Mr. Dilbagh Man Mohan Singh, is a trusted name in the marketing and distribution of pharmaceutical formulations, medical and non-medical devices, nutraceuticals, vaccines, surgical equipment, and hospital products. With a strong commitment to quality and innovation, Ameidic is dedicated to delivering cutting-edge in-vitro diagnostic devices, kits, and instruments that enhance healthcare and improve lives.

    Mr. Dilbagh Man Mohan Singh brings with him over 45 years of expertise in pharmaceuticals (Ethical & OTC), medical devices, and herbal products. His career spans senior managerial roles at Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd. (India) and Zydus Cadila across Vietnam, Philippines, Thailand, Cambodia, and other emerging markets.

    A visionary entrepreneur, he has successfully built long-standing relationships with global business associates and customers. Under his leadership, Ameidic Biotech continues to uphold the highest standards of reliability, trust, and innovation, while also being supported internationally by its sister concern, SSD Global Pte Ltd, Singapore.
