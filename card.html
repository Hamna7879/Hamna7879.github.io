Overview

Hepatitis B infection is a significant global health burden, often progressing to chronic liver disease, cirrhosis, or hepatocellular carcinoma. The HEALLINET HBsAg Rapid Test provides a critical front-line screening solution using a sandwich lateral flow immunochromatographic technique.

The assay utilizes high-affinity monoclonal and polyclonal antibodies to specifically capture HBsAg in serum, plasma, or whole blood samples. This methodology ensures high analytical sensitivity and diagnostic specificity, crucial for population-level screening and blood safety management in clinical environments where laboratory infrastructure may be limited.
